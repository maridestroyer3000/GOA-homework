Miss Me Jeans is a Los Angeles-based fashion brand launched in 2001, known for its bold, embellished denim.

The brand quickly gained popularity for its signature rhinestone and embroidered pocket designs, especially among women who wanted jeans with a standout,
glam-rock look. Miss Me also expanded into other apparel like dresses, tops, and jackets, but its detailed jeans remain the iconic centerpiece of the brand.
